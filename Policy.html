Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 22, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us when you create an account, such as your name, email address, and any neural data you process through our API.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used to provide, maintain, and improve our neural services. We do NOT use your private data to train public AI models without explicit consent.

3. Data Security

We implement AES-256 bit military-grade encryption to safeguard your information. Our decentralized storage ensures that your data remains secure even in the event of a localized breach.

4. Cookies

NexGen Core uses cookies to enhance your dashboard experience and remember your preferences. You can manage cookie settings through your browser at any time.

5. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We only share information with third-party providers necessary for payment processing or cloud infrastructure.
